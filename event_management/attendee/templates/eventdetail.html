Event Speakers

Here are some of our speakers

Nirvair pannu

Nirvair Pannu is a 26-year-old Punjabi singer-songwriter from India who’s quickly making his mark on the music industry. His sound combines traditional Punjabi folk music with modern hip hop and R&B influences, creating a unique blend of sounds that reflect his own unique style.

Gurnam Bhullar

Gurnam Bhullar is an Indian singer, actor, and lyricist known for his contributions to Punjabi music and films. He gained popularity with hit songs and has established himself as a prominent figure in the Indian pop music scene.
